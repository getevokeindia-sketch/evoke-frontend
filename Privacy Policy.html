Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Evoke Reminders collects only the minimal information required to deliver reminders (phone number, message templates, scheduling metadata). We do not sell data. For business verification, we may share business identity documents with Meta for app verification.

Evoke Reminders ("Evoke", "we", "our", "us") provides a WhatsApp-based reminder automation service that allows users to schedule and receive reminders. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use Evoke.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, mobile number.
  • Reminder Data: Dates, times, notes, or messages you create for reminders.
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, IP address, usage logs.
  • Third-Party Data: Information needed for WhatsApp message delivery through approved providers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver scheduled WhatsApp reminders.
  • Improve platform performance and user experience.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse or spam.

3. Sharing of Information

Your information may be shared only in the following cases:

  • WhatsApp Message Providers: Such as Meta Cloud API, AiSensy, or other approved partners needed for sending reminders.
  • Legal Requirements: If required to comply with law or protect our rights.
  • Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, and technical support partners.

We do NOT sell or trade your personal data.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your data. However, no online platform can guarantee 100% security.

5. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • View the personal data we hold.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Deactivate your Evoke account.

To request: email us at getevokeindia@gmail.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

7. Third-Party Platforms

Evoke uses third-party messaging providers (Meta WhatsApp Cloud API or approved partners) to deliver your reminders. These providers may independently collect or process your data according to their own Privacy Policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Evoke is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when required. The latest version will always be available on our website.
